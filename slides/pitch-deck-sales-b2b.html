Insurance & Liability

Six layers of protection.
You're covered.

The obvious question: what happens if the market crashes and collateral is insufficient? Anduin operates a six-layer insurance waterfall — your exposure as a broker is zero at every layer.

Anduin is liable for settlement shortfalls, not you. Your integration contract makes this explicit. You call batchSettle(); if the vault can't cover, the insurance waterfall absorbs the gap.

Protections are on-chain and auditable — not a promise in a PDF.

1
User Collateral (first loss)

User's own posted ETH/USDC/WBTC absorbs their own losses first. ~80% of cases end here.

2
5% Overcollateralisation Buffer

Vault maintains 105% collateral-to-debt at all times. Buffer absorbs sudden price gaps.

3
Anduin Insurance Fund

Protocol-owned treasury seeded from settlement fees. Covers flash crash scenarios.

4
Venue Guarantee Stakes

Exchanges that integrate Anduin optionally post a stake. Higher stake → lower fee tier. Skin in the game.

5
Reinsurance Partner

Third-party reinsurer backstops systemic risk events above the fund threshold.

6
Socialised Loss (last resort)

Extreme tail risk only. Pro-rata haircut across all creditors. Never triggered in normal operations.
